HOW TO PLAY

2-6 Players can play. Shuffle and deal equally. Each player places their decks face down and draws 5 cards. Choose one card to play. The player with the highest Latitude wins the battle and places all the cards played at the bottom of his deck. Going down the list of statistics, do the same for Longitude, No. of Stores and No. of Floors. If any battle is drawn, the cards are placed in the centre and will be won by the winner of the next battle. This is Phase 1. Draw 4 cards to replenish the ones played. Now, repeat the process, but using the bottom 4 statistics instead (Nearby Halal Stores, Retail Area, Nearby Bus Services and Opening Year). This is Phase 2. Phases 3 and 4 are similar, but the lowest statistic wins instead. Repeat these 4 phases. Should any player be unable to draw 4 cards, they will be eliminated. Last player standing wins.
